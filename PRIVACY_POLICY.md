# Grid Crown Block Blast Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 13, 2026

Grid Crown Block Blast ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data may be processed when you use the app.

## 1. Data We May Process

- Gameplay data stored locally on your device (for example score, settings, progress).
- Optional in-app profile values you enter for leaderboard use (for example nickname/country code), if that feature is enabled.
- Advertising-related data handled by ad providers (for example device identifiers, ad interaction signals) to deliver and measure ads.
- Purchase status (for example "remove ads" entitlement) to unlock paid features.

## 2. How Data Is Used

- To run core gameplay and save progress.
- To provide ads and ad performance measurement.
- To validate in-app purchases and restore entitlements.
- To operate optional online features such as leaderboards.

## 3. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party SDKs/services, including:

- Google AdMob (ads)
- Apple In-App Purchase services
- Firebase services (for optional backend/leaderboard features)

These providers may process data according to their own privacy policies.

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be shared with service providers only as needed to deliver app functionality (ads, purchases, optional online features).

## 5. Data Retention

- Local gameplay data remains on your device unless you remove the app or clear app data.
- Third-party service retention is governed by each provider's policy.

## 6. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children under the age required by local law without parental permission. If you believe a child provided data improperly, contact us.

## 7. Your Choices

- You can stop data processing by uninstalling the app.
- On supported platforms, you can manage tracking/advertising permissions in system settings.
